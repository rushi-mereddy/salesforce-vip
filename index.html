Program Overview

“Towards achieving a 50% female workforce by 2047”

In his Independence Day speech at the Red Fort, Prime Minister Narendra Modi highlighted the potential of women's empowerment to help India achieve its goal of becoming a developed nation. He later proposed the use of flexible workplaces to boost female participation in the workforce, with the ultimate aim of achieving a 50% female workforce by 2047.


To contribute to the goal of achieving 50% female workforce by 2047, SmartBridge and Salesforce have collaborated to provide Virtual Internship Program for female students and women with necessary knowledge and skills to excel in the job market and navigate the Salesforce ecosystem. The program aims to foster proficiency in the Salesforce domain, enabling them to become experts in this field.
